Lark Mason’s Preliminary Thoughts on Asia Week 2013

The sales are not yet finished, but this year Asia Week New York seems to be headed towards a solid performance. Over 40 dealers and galleries participated this year and five sponsor auction houses, resulting in a combined focus on Asian Art that arguably exceeds that of anywhere else in the world. The program’s cultural events have proven popular with the thousands of visitors to New York City, and the updated and improved Asia Week website has been of enormous benefit to out out of town guests.
